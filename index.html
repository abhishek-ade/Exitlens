Applicable Indian Labour Laws & Compliance Flags

POW
Payment of Wages Act, 1936
No salary increment for 2 years despite positive performance reviews. Timely and fair wage revision obligations may have been violated, especially without a formal review process.
FAC
Factories Act, 1948
Regular 12-hour shifts without overtime pay and poor factory ventilation are direct violations of Sections 51–54 (working hours) and Section 13 (ventilation and temperature standards).
IDA
Industrial Disputes Act, 1947
Unresolved worker grievances about supervisory aggression and HR inaction may constitute unfair labour practices under Schedule V of the Act.
POS
POSH Act, 2013
Supervisory aggression and workplace hostility must be evaluated under POSH for hostile work environment liability, regardless of whether the conduct is explicitly sexual in nature.
OSH
Occupational Safety, Health & Working Conditions Code, 2020
Overtime without double-rate compensation and poor ventilation are direct violations under this new Labour Code — which supersedes and consolidates earlier safety legislation.

HR must treat this not as one resignation but as an early warning signal requiring immediate systemic intervention across compensation, safety, and grievance handling.
